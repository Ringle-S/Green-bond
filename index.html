Frequently Asked Questions

A frequently asked question (FAQ) is a question that is commonly asked about a product, service, or website.

How often should I change my plant’s potting mix?

Small doses of nutrient-rich fertilizer during the growing season may also be enough to get your potting mix back in tip top shape.

Why are there gnats flying around my plants?

These insects feed on the fungus. To lose the gnats, let your plant’s potting mix completely dry out and work-in to the top layer some diatomaceous earth.

My flowers are falling off — is my plant dying?

No — flowers are just reproductive structures — they come and they go! They fall off when your plant is no longer feeling frisky.

How do I know if my plant is happy in its new home?

Don’t be alarmed if a plant doesn’t grow right away or do much. It may be in a winter dormancy period.
